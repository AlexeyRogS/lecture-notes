4.1 The 3D Quantum Harmonic Oscillator

Solving the quantum harmonic oscillator in one dimension proved to be quite a slog, but we will see that in more than one dimension it is much easier! This is because we can use the method of separation of variables to break down the problem into copies of the one-dimensional problem, for which we already know the solution.
